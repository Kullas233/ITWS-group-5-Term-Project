What is Flexicash?

Flexicash is the latest and greatest financial management application that is easy to understand and even easier to use. Once you're logged into your account, you can use our transaction management tools to log all of your expenses and earnings. After you've made some entries, you can see what's going on with your money by clicking on the "Check My Spending" button. There, you will be able to choose from a variety of of visual representations of where your money is coming from, as well as where it is going, providing you with the best information you can have to better manage your finances.
